Campus Life

School Field

Was established in 2013

very good sports field which can be used for either training or playing football, volleyball and track n field events during sports and games periods and for sports club activities in the evening.



School library

Was built in 2014

The superbly well stocked library houses a vast collection of books, digital sources, resources and information on a wide range of topics.



School Auditorium

Was built in 2013

The built up area of our school auditorium is 2240 sq. mts. It can accommodate up to 1700 individuals. There are 13 washrooms and 2 green rooms.



School Achievements

Inter House Sports competition
Won second in inter house football competition

The four houses are Coral (Red) Amber (Yellow) Jasper (Green) Beryl ( Blue). Every student on admission is placed in one of these houses. Inter House competitions are conducted on a regular basis to keep children exposed to current topics including global and environmental issues.

Inter House Swimming Competition
It is an inter house competition

Swimming is an important part of the curriculum. We have a 25Mts. pool equipped with eight lanes with temperature control and changing facility separately for boys and girls. The pool allows the young advanced level and basic level swimmers to learn the basics and advanced swimming skills in a hygienic and safe environment.
